Section for the text why your SRMS is needed.

A student result management system is an essential tool for educational institutions to efficiently manage and organize student academic records. It automates the process of recording and calculating grades, generating report cards, and tracking student progress over time. With features like secure data storage, easy access for administrators, teachers, and students, and the ability to generate detailed analytical reports, such systems streamline administrative tasks and enhance communication between stakeholders. Ultimately, they contribute to a more effective educational environment by providing valuable insights into student performance and facilitating informed decision-making by educators and administrators.
